Wind Breaker

Jay, a high school student, leads a double life as a skilled downhill biker. Balancing school and extreme sports, he navigates friendships, rivalries, and adrenaline-packed races. "Wind Breaker" captures the thrill of pushing limits while exploring the bonds formed through shared passion for biking.

Episodes: 24

Japanese: ウィンドブレイカー
